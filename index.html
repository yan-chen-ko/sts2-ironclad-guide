Slay the Spire 2 — Ironclad Cheat Sheet (Patch 0.98.1)

Last updated: 22 Mar 2026

Meta Snapshot

  • Ironclad = S-tier thanks to Burning Blood sustain, brutal Strength scaling and stronger Exhaust synergies compared to STS1. (RogueMeta)
  • Vulnerable archetype is its own lane now (Molten Fist, Tremble, Taunt, Dismantle) while Body Slam and Exhaust builds feel very familiar. (PCGamesN)
  • Combos: Hellraiser + Pommel Strike loops, Juggernaut + Body Slam block-kills, Anger/Headbutt recursion. (GameRant)
  • Core archetypes (Strength, Block, Exhaust, Bloodletting, Strike/infinite) still clear high Ascensions with proper drafting. (Mobalytics / TheGamer)

How This Tier List Was Built

  1. Cross-referenced FRVR top-card rankings, GameRant combo guides, PCGamesN build write-ups, Mobalytics + TheGamer archetype explanations and RogueMeta tier lists.
  2. Cards graded by standalone power, contribution across archetypes, and setup required.
  3. Only Early Access Patch 0.98.1 data considered.

Card Tier List

S Tier — Auto-picks / Backbone Cards

Corruption — Free skills + forced Exhaust = instant deck thinning and Feel No Pain/Dark Embrace win-cons. (FRVR / Mobalytics)
Offering — Burst draw + energy; in Bloodletting plans also procs Rupture. (FRVR / GameRant)
Hellraiser — Auto-plays all Strikes; pair with Pommel Strike/Pillage for infinite loops. (FRVR / GameRant)
Body Slam — Win button for Block decks; upgrade to 0 cost ASAP. (FRVR / PCGamesN)
Rupture — Converts self-damage (Brand, Fight Me!, Offering, Inferno) into permanent Strength. (FRVR / TheGamer)
Feel No Pain — Every Exhaust becomes Block; completes the Corruption/Dark Embrace triangle. (Mobalytics)

A Tier — Powerful With Light Setup

Pommel Strike — Cheap damage + draw, key for Hellraiser/Strike cycles. (GameRant)
Battle Trance — 0-cost draw-three to find win cons. (GameRant)
Juggernaut — Converts defense to damage, pairs with Block builds. (GameRant)
Molten Fist — Vulnerable payoff for aggro plans. (PCGamesN)
Tremble — Two turns of Vulnerable for 1 energy. (PCGamesN)
Taunt — Adds Block + Vulnerable, patching aggro defense. (PCGamesN)
Dark Embrace — Exhaust = draw; staple for Corruption. (Mobalytics)
Inflame — Efficient permanent Strength gain. (TheGamer)
Fight Me! — Huge Strength boost in Bloodletting shells; watch the enemy buff. (TheGamer)

B Tier — Situational / Needs Commitment

Demon Form — Wins long fights but slow at 3 energy. (TheGamer)
Barricade — Block archetype currently ranks a tier lower than Strength/Exhaust. (RogueMeta)
Dominate — Only worth it if you already stack Vulnerable. (PCGamesN)
Perfected Strike — Needs a dedicated Strike deck, which sits below top-tier plans. (RogueMeta)
Brand / Bloodletting package — Great with Rupture + healing, risky without. (TheGamer)

Run-Level Tips

  • Don’t force a build before the Act I boss. Pick best-in-slot cards and pivot once you see relics/boss rewards. (RogueMeta)
  • Card removal > random additions. Lean decks find their win-cons more often. (RogueMeta)
  • Plan branching acts. Overgrowth vs Underdock nodes change elites/events; chart gold & shops. (RogueMeta)
  • Combo reminders:
    • Hellraiser + Pommel Strike/Pillage = free auto-attacks once the deck is filtered. (GameRant)
    • Juggernaut + Flame Barrier/Unmovable = damage per block tick, great vs Spikers. (GameRant)
    • Corruption + Feel No Pain + Dark Embrace + Body Slam = infinite defense + nukes. (Mobalytics)
